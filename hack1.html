Every young
person
deserves a
good
childhood

That's why we fight for the
hope and happiness of young
people when it's threatened
by abuse, exploitation and
neglect. We see the hope and
courage in young people
every day, and it inspires us to
support them through their
most serious life challenges.

Now we're stepping up the
fight for hope and happiness
at a time when it's under
threat like never before. Our
goal and vision are built
around the hopes of young
people, to achieve long-lasting
change for this generation
and the ones to come. Find
out who we are.

In their
own words



When young people want to tell their
story, we listen. We see something in
every one of them. These young people
still believe in a better tomorrow. They
know they can get away from those who
hurt them, have a happy home life, feel
better over time.



Often their stories aren't easy to tell,
but they tell them. Because they know
sharing their story will help them and
others like them.

Our Impact

Last year we worked with more than
11,500 young people through direct
practice. These young people have a
huge impact on us - they shine a light
on parts of society that is broken for
them, and in turn we help them regain
their hope, even when it's hanging by a
thread.
